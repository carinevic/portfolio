About Me

I am a full-stack developer living in Houston,TX. I obtained my HND in Marketing from National Polytechnic Bambui in Cameroon. After my HND I worked as a manager for ARA a roofing company for 2 years before moving to the USA. I obtained my Associate Degree in computer science from Houston Community college. After three uninspiring years studying nursing, I needed something challenging. As an avid online shopper, I was intrigued by the process from picking out items to checking out, and the coding involved. After watching a couple of coding videos on youtube and reading Java for Dummies, I decided Software development was for me.
